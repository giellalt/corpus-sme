Skábmajazz Anáris: Sámeartisttaid násttit lávddi alde Sajosis Famous Sámi Musicians in Sajos During Kaamosjazz Festival
Skábmajazz fállá harmonalaš eahketkonsearttaid, heahkas jamiid sihke sámemusihkkai deattuhuvvan eahkeda Sámekulturguovddáš Sajosis Anáris skábmamánu 21. beaivve 2015 dmu 17. Sámeartisttaid násttit Hilda Länsman ja Wimme Saari loaiddasteaba bändiin Sajosa konsearttas. Famous traditional Sámi artists Hilda Länsman and Wimme Saari will perform in Sámi Cultural Center Sajos auditorium in Inari on Saturday 21.11.2015.
Bivnnut juoigi Wimme Saari lea badjánan málmmibeakkánin laktimiin modearna mášenelemeanttaid boares juoiganteknihkaidda. Joik artist Wimme Saari has become world famous with his original style in traditional Sámi music.
Wimme juigosiin leat nannosit ovdan olbmot, eallit ja luondu. In his music he mixes old joiking technics with modern electronic elements.
Su lassin Sajosa lávddi alde loaiddasta Hilda Länsman. Nature, animals and people are strongly present in his joiks.
Son lea 22-jahkásaš artista ja lávllaráhkadeaddji, guhte bođii dovddusin UMK-lávlungilvvu mielde giđđat 2015, go loaiddastii euroviisurátkimiid finálas ovttas etniinis Ulla Pirttijärvi-Länsmaniin. Hilda Länsman is a young Sámi singer and song writer, who became known in Finland, when she took part in Eurovision qualification song contest UMK, with her mother Ulla Pirttijärvi-Länsman, in spring 2015.
