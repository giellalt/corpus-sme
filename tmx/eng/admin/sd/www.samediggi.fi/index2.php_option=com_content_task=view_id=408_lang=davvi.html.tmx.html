Kongreassa- ja dáhpáhusbálvalusat Conference and Event Services
Sámekulturguovddáža áššehasbálvalanovttadaga, Kongreassa- ja dáhpáhusbálvalusaid, bargun lea vástidit guovddáža oktasašsajiid márkanastimis, vuovdimis ja várremiin, fuolahit dáhpáhus- ja čoahkkinlágidemiin ja dikšut teknihkka-, informašuvdna- ja fáktameašttirbálvalusaid. Conference and Event Services is responsible of marketing, sales and reservations for the centre ’ s common facilities, handling of the arrangements for events and conferences, and management of the centre ’ s technology, information and security services.
Kongreassa- ja dáhpáhusbálvalusaid sajit leat kulturguovddáža vuosttas dásis, váldofeaskára ja čoahkkinsajiid oktavuođas. The Conference and Event Services office is located on the first floor of the cultural centre, adjacent to the lobby and conference rooms.
Lassedieđu sámekulturguovddáš Sajosa oktasašsajiin, maid sáhttá láigohit, oaččot Sajosa ruoktosiidduin. More information about the reservations of the premises from here.
