Geavaheaddji sáhttá válljet guđe fáttá dahje oahppogirjji sániiguin háliida bargat Leksas ja Morfas. The user can choose to work with words from a specific topic or textbook, in Leksa and in Morfa-S.
Dát sátnevuorká geavahuvvo aktiivvalaččat min prográmmain, muhto Vasta- ja Sahka-prográmmain geavaheaddji sáhttá geavahit ollu eanet sániid. The vocabulary is used actively in our programs, but in Vasta and Sahka the user may use more words.
Prográmma geavaha dalle leksikonaid main leat oktiibuot 100.000 sáni (daid gaskkas leat sullii 40.000 nama). The program then uses a lexicon with appr. 100.000 words (among them appr. 40.000 names).
Vuolábealde leat sátnelisttut main leat substantiivvat, vearbbat, advearbbat ja adjektiivvat seahkálaga, ja bordojuvvon alfabehtalaččat. Below you will find wordlists for nouns, verbs and adjectives, ordered alphabetically.
Daid sáhttá geavahit oahpahusas máŋgga ládje, ja de oahppit sáhttet iskat máhtuset Leksas dahje Morfas. These may be used for teaching in different ways, and then the pupils may test their skills in Morfa and Leksa afterwards.
Go lea word-fiilan, de lea álki listtuid redigeret. The files are stored in Word format, for ease of manipulation.
Juohkin lea sullii seammá go Leksa-prográmmas, muhto das leat juohke sánis eanet vejolaš jorgalusat go sátnelisttuin. The division is approximately as in Leksa, but Leksa accepts more translations than what is included in the lists.
Dáin listtuin lea dárogiela jorgalus: The number of lists will be enlarged.
