Aimo Guttorm válljejuvvui Sámedikki láhkačálli virgái Aimo Guttorm appointed Legal Secretary
Ohcejohkalaš vuoigatvuohtadiehtaga kandidáhtta / várreduopmár Aimo Guttorm válljejuvvui Sámedikki láhkačálli fásta virgái. Aimo Guttorm was appointed Legal Secretary for Saami Parliament in Finland 2.6.2008.
