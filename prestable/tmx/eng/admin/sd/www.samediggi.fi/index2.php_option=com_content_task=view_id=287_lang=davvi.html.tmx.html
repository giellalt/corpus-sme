Sámedikki almmolaš doaimmahagas ohcanláhkai leamašan kulturčálli virgesadjásažžan lea váldojuvvon musihka magisttar Anna Näkkäläjärvi. Anna Näkkäläjärvi as deputy for the Cultural Secretary
Näkkäläjärvi álggaha barggustis miessemánu 18. beaivve 2009. Su doaibmabáiki lea Sámedikki almmolaš doaimmahagas Anára Rivdolis. Master of Music Anna Näkkäläjärvi has been appointed as deputy for the Cultural Secretary.
Virgesadjásašvuođa áigemeari rádjai ohce Anna Näkkäläjärvi lassin njeallje eará ohcci. Näkkäläjärvi will begin her work on 18 May in the Saami Parliaments General office in Inari.
Lassidieđut hálddahushoavda Juha Guttormis. Further information from Executive Director Juha Guttorm.
